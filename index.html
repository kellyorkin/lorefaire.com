The maker

Somewhere between a renaissance faire and a candlelit library, you'll find the spirit of Lore Faire — a maker's collection of handcrafted jewelry, wearable art, and curiosities born from D&D campaigns, woodland wanderings, and a deep love of the handmade.

Each piece carries its own story. Dragon claw pendants. Tarot charm bracelets. Celtic knotwork. Steampunk timepieces. Painted worlds. Miniature terrariums. No two quite the same — because that was never the point.
