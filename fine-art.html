A note from the maker

These pieces don't begin with a plan. They begin with a color, a found object, a character that won't leave the imagination alone. The jewelry and the paintings come from the same place — curiosity, obsession, and a deep unwillingness to stop at the expected medium.

If something calls to you, it was probably made for you.
